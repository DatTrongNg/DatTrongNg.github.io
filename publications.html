Towards Understanding and Quantifying Uncertainty for Text-to-Image Generation
Gianni Franchi, Dat Nguyen Trong, Nacim Belkhir, Guoxuan Xia, Andrea Pilzer
IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition, 2025  ·  arXiv:2412.03178
We propose a framework for understanding and quantifying uncertainty in text-to-image generative models, introducing a novel metric for evaluating how well a model captures the semantic concepts present in its training data.
