About me

I specialise in computer vision and generative AI — from training large-scale diffusion models (SDXL, Pixart-α/Σ) to deploying production-grade detection and segmentation pipelines. My work sits at the intersection of research and engineering: I care about models that actually work in the real world, at scale.

Before moving into ML, I worked as a software engineer at Tek Experts (Hanoi), maintaining backend services for Microsoft Dynamics 365. I then came to France on an IDEX scholarship to pursue a Master's in Machine Vision and AI at Université Paris-Saclay. During my internship at ENSTA Paris (U2IS Lab), my research on uncertainty quantification led to a CVPR 2025 publication.

I currently work as a Machine Learning Engineer at Carviz (Paris), building production computer vision systems for automated automotive inspection.

Fluent in Vietnamese and English.
