About me

I specialise in computer vision and generative AI — from training large-scale diffusion models (SDXL, Pixart-α/Σ) to deploying production-grade detection and segmentation pipelines. My work sits at the intersection of research and engineering: I care about models that actually work in the real world, at scale.

Before moving into ML, I worked as a software engineer at Tek Experts (Hanoi), maintaining backend services for Microsoft Dynamics 365. I then came to France on an IDEX scholarship to pursue a Master's in Machine Vision and AI at Université Paris-Saclay (ENSTA Paris), where my internship research led to a CVPR 2025 paper on uncertainty in text-to-image models.

Fluent in Vietnamese, English, and French.
